# AI Website Builder Briefing: Operation B2C Dominance

**TO:** Replit AI / Web Developer
**FROM:** The Video Guy
**SUBJECT:** Construction Protocol for the Bacho Iliya Digital Beachhead

## 1. PROJECT MISSION: This is Not a Website.

Before you write a single line of code, understand this: we are not building a corporate website. We are building an **intelligence-gathering weapon**.

- **Primary Objective:** To map the B2C market by capturing user location and preference data through an engaging, game-like interface.
- **Secondary Objective:** To build a pre-launch email list of highly motivated potential customers.
- **Cover Story:** The site is disguised as a teaser for a national movement called "Революцията на истинския вкус" (The Revolution of Real Taste).

Every design and content choice must serve this mission. We are not here to inform. We are here to provoke, engage, and extract data.

## 2. BRAND PERSONA: The Voice of Bacho Iliya

The website must speak with the voice of our brand persona, "Bacho Iliya."

- **Who He Is:** A wise, authentic, slightly cheeky guardian of Bulgarian tradition. He's not a corporate salesman; he's the village elder who has seen it all and isn't afraid to speak the truth. He is proud, honest, and has a sense of humor.
- **Tone of Voice:**
    - **Confident, not Arrogant:** Uses strong, declarative statements.
    - **Authentic, not Polished:** Language is direct and uses traditional Bulgarian sentiment. Avoid corporate jargon at all costs.
    - **Provocative, not Insulting:** Challenges the user's assumptions ("Войната срещу компромиса започна.") but always positions our brand as the hero fighting for them.

The AI must generate all supporting text and microcopy in this voice.

## 3. TARGET AUDIENCE PSYCHOLOGY

We are speaking to two primary mindsets. The design and user experience must appeal to their core drivers.

- **The Traditionalist Mom (45-55):**
    - **She Fears:** That the real taste she remembers from her childhood is gone forever.
    - **She Desires:** Authenticity, honesty, and a connection to her heritage.
    - **How We Win Her:** The website's visual aesthetic must feel rustic, warm, and traditional (natural textures like wood and embroidered cloth). The language must validate her frustration with modern, fake products.
- **The Health-Conscious Parent (28-40):**
    - **She Fears:** Hidden, unhealthy ingredients (palm oil, starch, chemicals) in the food she gives her family.
    - **She Desires:** Transparency, clean labels, and the peace of mind that comes from trust.
    - **How We Win Her:** The website must feel clean, trustworthy, and professional without being sterile. The copy must emphasize "100% мляко" and our "без компромиси" philosophy.

## 4. DESIGN & UX PHILOSOPHY: The Laws of Persuasion

The website's design and functionality must follow these ruthless principles of user experience and persuasion.

- **Principle 1: Clarity Over Cleverness (Jakob's Law)**
    - **Mandate:** The user must never be confused. The navigation (`ЗА НАС`, `РЕЦЕПТИ`, `ПРОДУКТИ`) must use simple, conventional labels. The user flow must be a straight line from the hook to the data capture form. Do not deviate.
- **Principle 2: Create an Irresistible Hook (Pitch Anything)**
    - **Mandate:** The first section of the website must be a powerful **pattern interrupt**. The cinematic video and provocative headline ("Революцията на истинския вкус започна.") are designed to create immediate intrigue and stop the user from scrolling past.
- **Principle 3: Simplify the Ask (Hick's Law)**
    - **Mandate:** The data-capture quiz in Section 2 must be frictionless. Limit the choices in each question to a maximum of 3-4 options. Too many choices will create decision fatigue and increase the drop-off rate. The path to submitting their email must be fast and easy.
- **Principle 4: Aesthetics Build Trust (Aesthetic-Usability Effect)**
    - **Mandate:** The website must *look* authentic. A beautiful, well-designed site is perceived as more trustworthy.
        - **Visual Style:** Use high-quality, professional photography. The aesthetic should be warm, rustic, and natural.
        - **Typography:** Use clean, classic fonts that are easy to read.
        - **Color Palette:** Earthy tones, natural colors. Avoid bright, garish "marketing" colors.

This is the strategic context. Ensure the AI understands these principles before it generates a single pixel. This is how we ensure the final product is a weapon, not just another website.
